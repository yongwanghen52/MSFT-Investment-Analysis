## Microsoft Corporation (MSFT) Investment Analysis

### Project Overview

This repository hosts a comprehensive investment research project for **Microsoft Corporation (MSFT)** as part of a semester portfolio management course. The project includes data collection, financial analysis, valuation modeling, and reporting to support investment decision-making.

### Research Purpose & Methodology

- **Purpose:** Evaluate Microsoft’s financial health, market position, and growth prospects to determine its suitability for inclusion in a diversified portfolio.
- **Methodology:**
  - Collect fundamental financial data (price, sector, market cap, income statement, balance sheet).
  - Analyze institutional ownership and analyst sentiment.
  - Review recent news and dividend history.
  - Perform quantitative analysis and qualitative assessment.
